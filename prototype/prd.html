01Overview

Fashion's first prediction market.

High Street is a platform where users compete on fashion outcomes — trend lifecycles, runway results, designer moves, and product drops. Exchange-grade interaction patterns adapted for a play-mode CREDS economy.

Every prediction generates a demand signal. Consumer behavior feeds back into FI's B2B graph, making enterprise intelligence stronger while High Street compounds a consumer reputation moat.

CREDS-first Play Mode Status + Access Incentives Powered by FI AI Stack Cash Mode as Separate Track
02Problem

Three gaps. One platform.

01
Fashion Consumers
Opinions with no upside

Everyone on fashion TikTok makes trend predictions. No platform lets them prove that edge through durable rankings, streaks, and public credibility. It lives in comments and dies there.

02
Brands & Buyers
Forecasting is slow and expensive

WGSN charges $50K+/year for reports from small analyst panels. There's no real-time consumer consensus — the signal that actually matters.

03
Trend Agencies
Data from the wrong people

Panels of 20 analysts can't match the aggregate signal of 50,000 fashion consumers actively pricing probabilities through repeat participation and visible track records.

High Street — Product Thesis

FI tells brands this trend has 8 weeks of runway. High Street lets consumers compete on it.

04Market Types & Resolution

Four types. Zero ambiguity.

Every market has pre-defined resolution criteria set before trading opens. The Trend Index oracle — built on FI's demand pipeline — is the advantage no competitor can replicate.

Type 01 — Factual
Designer & Industry Events
"Will Demna leave Balenciaga before 2027?"
Resolution Oracle
Official announcement via BoF or WWD press coverage
Type 02 — Event Outcome
Runway & Red Carpet
"Best dressed at Met Gala 2026?"
Resolution Oracle
Aggregate of Vogue, Harper's, Elle, WWD within 48h
Type 03 — Commercial
Drops & Collabs
"Nike × Jacquemus sells out in 24 hours?"
Resolution Oracle
Official brand / retailer sold-out status
Type 04 — Trend Index ✦ FI-Powered
Trend Lifecycle Markets
"Quiet luxury still dominant by Fall 2026?"
Resolution Oracle
High Street Trend Index — composite of search, social, editorial, retail — powered by FI's AI stack
