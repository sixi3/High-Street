01

ONE-LINE THESIS

VantageX turns top fashion resellers into public tastemakers and gives consumers a way to follow, co-sign, and act on their conviction — powered by a trend intelligence engine that predicts resale-price direction better than historical comps alone.

What Exists Today

  • Reseller tools are operational — listing, cross-posting, inventory sync. No intelligence.
  • Marketplaces show past prices but zero forward-looking signal.
  • Consumer discovery is search/grid-based, not creator-led.

What VantageX Is

Reseller Side
Supply
AI trend intelligence, portfolio analytics, timing signals, public profile, credibility score. Resellers are ranked creators with track records.
Consumer Side
Demand
Follow resellers, discover trends through people, co-sign picks with virtual credits, buy physical items through linked marketplaces.
02

PROBLEM & OPPORTUNITY

Global secondhand apparel is projected to reach $393B by 2030 (ThredUp 2026). An estimated 200K-500K serious resellers make repeated sourcing and pricing decisions that directly impact income.

Three Daily Decisions

SOURCE
What should I buy to resell next?
TIMING
Should I sell now or hold for better price?
PRICE
What's the right number today?

Current tools answer these with stale comps and intuition. VantageX adds forward signals plus social proof.

03

USERS & JOBS TO BE DONE

Primary ICP: Professional Resellers

  • 100+ active listings across multiple platforms
  • Multi-platform sellers (StockX, Grailed, Depop, Vestiaire, eBay, Instagram)
  • Need an edge in sourcing, timing, and pricing
Reseller Jobs
  • Find rising trends early — before the rest of the market catches on
  • Avoid late-cycle inventory traps — don't source items in declining trends
  • Improve realized margin per item — sell closer to peak, not too early or too late
  • Build audience and reputation — not just transactions, but credibility

Secondary: Fashion Consumers & Trend Followers

Consumer Jobs
  • Find trusted curators — follow tastemakers, not catalogs
  • Discover what's rising — before it's mainstream
  • Participate in competitive prediction loops — express conviction through lightweight gameplay

HOW USERS "MAKE MONEY"

Year 1: Play-Money Economy

  • Users earn credits through participation, streaks, and accuracy
  • Credits improve rank, unlock badges, and gated platform perks
  • No direct cash redemption in Year 1

Year 2+: Real-Money Markets (Regulated Path Only)

CONTRACT MECHANICS ──────────────────────────────────────── Format: Binary outcome (UP or DOWN) Price range: $0.00 to $1.00 Example trade: You buy UP at $0.62 ← 62% implied probability Trend resolves higher ← FI score increased Payout: $1.00 ← Full resolution Gross gain: $0.38/share ← Before fees You may also close position before expiration as prices move with new information. ⚠ Requires: regulated infrastructure, KYC/AML, surveillance, resolution governance.
06

MARKET DESIGN

Not every market should be 90 days.

30D
Short Cycle
Higher engagement frequency. More trades per user. Noisier signal — requires sufficient liquidity.
90D
Standard Cycle
Better alignment with fashion trend movement. Reduces noise. Primary contract at launch.

Resolution Principle

  • Baseline signal recorded at market open
  • Final signal recorded at resolution timestamp
  • Outcome rules versioned and auditable
  • If real-money: resolution governance must be independently auditable

GO-TO-MARKET

Months 0-3
Validation + Founding 100
Recruit 100 resellers across archetypes (archive, sneakers, contemporary, niche, indie). Free Pro access. Collect feedback on recommendation utility. Publish first accuracy report. Backtest FI against 12-18 months of historical resale data.
Months 3-6
Open Reseller Launch
Creator partnerships with reseller YouTubers/TikTok. Discord/Reddit community loops. Import tooling to reduce onboarding friction. Target: 5,000 resellers, 1,250 paying.
Months 6-12
Consumer Layer Expansion
Public profiles, follow graph, feed ranking. Co-sign credits, streaks, seasons, badges. Enhanced affiliate commerce flows. Play-money trend markets for engagement.

KEY RISKS

CRITICAL
Signal Validity
Trend signals fail to predict resale-price direction.
Mitigation: Backtest + ongoing public accuracy reports. Model augmentation with transactional price feeds. If R² < 0.3, do not launch.
HIGH
Data Access / Platform Dependency
Source disruption from ToS/API policy changes on Instagram, TikTok, StockX, etc.
Mitigation: Source diversification, legal review, modular ingestion stack. No single-platform dependency.
HIGH
Cold Start
Low reseller participation weakens social/feed utility.
Mitigation: Founding 100 program, creator onboarding, importer-first UX. Resellers bring existing audiences.
MEDIUM
Trust / Gamification Integrity
Score gaming or low-quality co-sign behavior.
Mitigation: Anti-abuse rules, quality-weighted ranking, season resets, transparent methodology.
MEDIUM-HIGH
Regulatory (Future Real-Money Layer)
Compliance burden, restricted market access for prediction contracts.
Mitigation: Year 1 is play-money only. Explore partner-led regulated deployment later. Dream11 regulatory experience directly applicable.
